I built an SEO toolkit for Claude Code and put it out as open source.

Seven skills and three agents that turn Claude Code into an SEO analyst you already have open: full site audits, single page reviews, technical checks, content quality against E-E-A-T, strategy, and competitor comparison pages. It also checks for GEO, how a site shows up in AI Overviews, ChatGPT and Perplexity, not just classic Google ranking.

The idea is simple. Point it at a URL, it crawls, checks and reasons about the site in the same terminal session you're already working in. No separate login, no dashboard, no exporting to a spreadsheet to make sense of it.

For a full audit it crawls up to 500 pages, works out what kind of site it's looking at (SaaS, ecommerce, local business, publisher, agency), then fans the work out to specialist agents running in parallel so it comes back with one health score and a prioritised list, not three reports you have to reconcile yourself.

MIT licensed, free to clone and use.
